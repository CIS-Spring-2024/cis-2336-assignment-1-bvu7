About Us/Biography

Kyle (Owner and Head Chef)

As "Kyle the Great," I'm deeply rooted in Sugarland, where I've poured my passion for culinary arts into Cougar Cafe since its inception in 2010. From managing daily operations to fostering a supportive work environment for my team, my dedication to excellence has earned respect and admiration throughout the University of Houston. Through Cougar Cafe, I've not only crafted a beloved local institution but also embraced philanthropy, embodying the transformative power of passion and hard work in shaping the university's culture.
